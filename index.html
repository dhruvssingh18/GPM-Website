Grade Plus Math

I had been using online subscription to teach my son for the past few months. He was doing the assignments mostly on his own and I used to guide him and monitor his progress. Later I started a math club with him and few of his friends. I had prepared 20 questions to test him and his friends. I was expecting my son to do well in my test since he was familiar with a variety of topics but was surprised to see that he got only 80% correct.


His result got me thinking about the mistakes. Was he in a rush to solve the problems? Was he tensed? Since the questions were on various topics, did he have problem context switching? Had he forgotten the topic that he learnt earlier? Was it something else? I started giving them more tests to check their progress periodically and also how well they do in the test environment and I saw lot of improvement.


I looked online for test questions and it was hard to find many that matches their level and tested all their grade level topics in one single set. Quickly I exhausted all the online options. Later I started preparing the questions myself. Since this has been useful to them, I thought of making it available for others too. I plan to readjust the questions based on overall test results over the course of time. As my son grew older, he became more ingested in Grade Plus Math and dedicated in is free time to engaging trivia & math competitions to engage and grasp a larger audience. If you have suggestions for improvement please don't hesitate to get in touch with me.

-Dhruv Singh & Satish Singh(Co-founders)
